Frequently Asked Questions

Why am I seeing this page?

You're seeing this page because your device has lost internet connection or the app is unable to reach our servers. This is a normal part of how Progressive Web Apps work.

Can I still use the PDF Compressor offline?

Yes! The PDF Compressor works entirely offline. All processing happens directly on your device, so you can compress PDFs even without an internet connection.

How do I get back online?

Ensure your device is connected to the internet (Wi-Fi or mobile data). This page will automatically refresh when connectivity is restored. You can also manually try again using the button above.
